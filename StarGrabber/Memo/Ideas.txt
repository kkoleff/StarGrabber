0. Initialization. Chosing user's Name, Race, Specs, FirstShip name and Difficulty level.

1. The game starts on StarSystemSpace map which location on InterGalaxy map depends on race.

2. Initially player governs 1 planet

3. Every planet could have 3 type of structures: Factories, Military Structures and Homes which costs credits and resources.
	- Planet with factories can produce Ships and Weapons
	- Military Structures increase Defence points which gives better chance to defeat the attacker.
	- Homes are for population. To reach the Maximum population you will need homes. More population, faster production and better deffence

4. You can form a Fleet but smaller than 10 ships and command it as a whole.

5. Backgrounds have random stars and depends on the map context.
